Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 June 2026

This privacy policy explains how WebsitePal collects, uses and protects personal information when you enquire about, buy or use our website build, hosting and maintenance service.

Who We Are

WebsitePal provides website design, setup, hosting, maintenance and support for clients. For the purposes of data protection law, WebsitePal is the controller of the personal information described in this policy.

Contact email: info@websitepal.pro

Information We Collect

We may collect and use the following types of information:

  • Contact details such as your name, email address, phone number and company name.
  • Account details used to create and manage your WebsitePal portal account.
  • Business information you submit for your website, including services, areas covered, opening hours, team details, images, logos, social links and other content notes.
  • Messages, support requests and feedback you send to us.
  • Payment and billing information. Card payments are processed by Stripe and we do not store your full card details.
  • Technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, sign-in records and basic usage data needed to keep the portal secure and working properly.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • create and manage your account;
  • build, host, maintain and support your website;
  • prepare drafts, layouts, copy, image selections and website content based on the details you provide;
  • send service messages about your website build, payments, hosting, maintenance or support;
  • process payments, subscriptions, invoices and refunds;
  • keep the portal, websites and our systems secure;
  • meet legal, tax and accounting requirements;
  • improve our service and internal processes.

Our Lawful Bases

We use personal information where it is needed to perform our contract with you, where we have a legitimate interest in running and improving our service, where we need to comply with a legal obligation, or where you have given consent for a specific optional use.

Who We Share Information With

We only share personal information where it is needed to provide, secure or manage the service. This may include payment processors, hosting providers, database providers, email providers, analytics or security tools, domain and website service providers, professional advisers, and other suppliers who help us build and maintain client websites.

Where a supplier acts for us, we expect them to use the information only for the services they provide to WebsitePal.

International Transfers

Some service providers may process information outside the United Kingdom. Where this happens, we will take reasonable steps to make sure appropriate safeguards are in place.

How Long We Keep Information

We keep client account, project and website information for as long as needed to provide the service and manage the relationship. We may keep billing, tax and legal records for longer where required by law. Backups and archived records may take additional time to be fully removed from all systems.

Your Rights

Depending on the circumstances, you may have the right to ask for access to your personal information, correction of inaccurate information, deletion, restriction, objection to certain uses, data portability, or withdrawal of consent where consent is being used.

You can contact us at info@websitepal.pro. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office if you are unhappy with how your personal information is handled.

Cookies And Similar Technologies

The WebsitePal portal uses essential cookies or similar technologies to keep you signed in and keep the service secure. If we add optional analytics or marketing cookies in future, we will update this policy and provide any consent controls required.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will be published on this page.
